About Series

This series is taking place in an army medical center located in a remote military base and following the bizarre characters and occurrences in the center.

This show accurately portrays some of the events a soldier in Israel has to go through. It shows a different light on the great importance of one's service in the army, in addition to the stress and anxiety that is forced upon the medics whose other's lives are deeply relied upon.
